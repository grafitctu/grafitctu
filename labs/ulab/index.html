Usability lab

We help students and others better understand what user friendly means. We help detect flaws in the UI design and the service as such. We offer the possibility to test the results of projects and theses by working with real users. We help companies with testing of mobile apps, websites, digital assistants and many more. When not testing an app or website, we are thinking about what our interaction with computers will look like in the future.
