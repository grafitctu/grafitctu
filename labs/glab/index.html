The lab serves as the midpoint of the three graphics-related topics: Visualization and Recognition, Game design, and Arts.

The lab currently supports students' projects with an Interactive wall, Tactile Matrix Box, and many VR headsets (XTAL, HTC focus 3, HTC elite, Oculus Quest 2), all open for collaboration and development.
