Laboratories

    The Grafit research group manages four laboratories. The older and main is SAGElab, which is a joint workplace with the CESNET association and a long-term computer graphics center at FIT. The (children) usability lab is small but often used by students for testing their projects. The last ones are VR lab and Glab.

Research

SAGElab

The SAGElab is a joint workplace of the CESNET association and the Faculty of Information Technology of the Czech Technical University in Prague. The purpose of the laboratory is to support the teaching of subjects and education of professionals in the fields of network technologies, multimedia and virtual reality and to create an environment for research and development of new network applications.

  • Interactive 3D models with remote access
  • Live culture over the network
  • Data Visualization
  • Internet of Things

Usability lab

The lab focuses on the child user. Thanks to the acquired equipment allow developers, psychologists, and educators to observe the behavior of the target group.

  • UX testing
  • Children usability testing
  • Cooperation with SAGElab

Glab

The lab serves as the midpoint of the three graphics-related topics: Visualization, Recognition, and Arts.

  • graphics
  • game design
  • generative arts
