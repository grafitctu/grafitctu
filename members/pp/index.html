Founder of group. Assistant professor at FIT, CTU and FNSPE, CTU in Prague.

  • Founder of Grafit group
  • Computational fluid dynamics models
  • VR and AR
  • Fractals and their applications
  • MRI data visualization

is currently an assistant professor at Faculty of Information Technology, Czech Technical University in Prague He received his Ph.D.'s degree in Applied mathematics from the Faculty of Nuclear sciences and physical engineering, Czech Technical University in Prague. He is interested in the following fields: Computer graphics, Virtual and mixed reality, Visualization and segmentation of medical data (MRI/CT), Material science (dislocations in metal crystals).
