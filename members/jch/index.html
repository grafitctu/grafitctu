Ing. Jiří Chludil

X is currently an assistant professor at Faculty of Information Technology, Czech Technical University in Prague and postdoc on the Department of Recognition, Czech Academy of Sciences. He received his Ph.D.'s degree in Computer Graphics from the Faculty of Information Technology, Czech Technical University in Prague. His areas of interest cover dynamic texture modeling, game design, pattern recognition and computational geometry.
