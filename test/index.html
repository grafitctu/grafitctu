NI-CCC

nic: [in czech meaning nothing] a notion so simple, thus with an unlimited meaning [x]

NICCC is a study course organized at Faculty of Information Technology, Czech Technical University in Prague in cooperation with other institutions and individuals. The course is an open space for people from different fields - a merge of IT, architecture, urbanism, or simply any research field or art of any format. The goal is to support the creation of interdisciplinary projects - big screen visualization, physical installations, multimedial projects, etc. The main focus is to connect students and mentors with various sets of knowledge, skills, and fields of interest. The individuality of both students and the mentors is the foundation for unique projects, which are being developed throughout the semester.
