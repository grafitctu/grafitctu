30.11.2020 - Jiří Zemánek, Jan Netušil, and Jan Petýrek

Jiří Zemánek is a postdoctoral researcher at FEE CTU, he spent part of the postdoctoral studies at MIT at the Center for Bits and Atoms. His main research interests are intelligent materials and micromanipulation of objects by shaping physical force fields.

Jan Netušil is a graduate of the Academy of Arts, Architecture, and Design. Since 2012 he has been a member of Oficina and is a co-founder and technical director of the Mouvo festival.

Jan Petýrek is a graduate of the Academy of Arts, program MA Information Experience Design. He is a video artist with particular interest in perception of space and spatial narratives. His work explores virtual and physical experiences with an aim to create immersive installations.
