Gamejam 2022

The event, 48hour long hackaton, was primarily intended for students, yet anyone can apply. Here anyone had the opportunity to benefit from regular feedback provided by a few lecturers from FIT CTU, as well as some people from the game industry during the evaluation part, such as Warhorse Studios.

The event took place at CTU FIT, SAGElab and Glab and was held by Grafit members, FIT, CESNET, IPR, and students.

The created game was determined by 4 ingredients, from which 3 had to be used, which should be melded creatively together to create one game created in 48 hours.

  • small and big
  • mist
  • structure
  • per ardua ad astra
